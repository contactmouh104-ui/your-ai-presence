Step 04 — Anti-Spam Architecture

The Stealth Link Strategy

🔑
DM Keyword Automation
Use tools like ManyChat or Instagram's native DM automation. In your Reel CTA, say: "DM me the word BREAKOUT" — when they do, the bot instantly sends a personalized welcome + your bridge page link. Never post raw affiliate links publicly. The link only travels inside DMs — Instagram cannot flag it as spam because it's a private conversation triggered by user intent.
🌉
"Link in Bio" Bridge Page
Never link directly to JVZoo from your bio. Instead, build a simple Linktree-style bridge page (use Carrd, Notion, or a custom domain) that lists all 5 products with short descriptions and your affiliate links behind clean buttons. Instagram sees a legitimate website — not a raw affiliate URL. The bridge page also pre-sells the visitor before they hit the offer, increasing conversions by 30–50%.
🎯
Comment Funnel with "Reply Trigger"
Post a Reel and in the caption write: "Comment 'INFO' below and I'll drop the link for you." When users comment, your DM automation triggers and sends them the bridge page. This creates massive engagement (comments = algorithm fuel) while keeping your link distribution 100% in DMs. Triple win: engagement boost + link safety + personalized delivery.
📧
The Email Capture Play
Your bridge page should include a simple opt-in: "Get the Full Free Strategy Guide" in exchange for an email. Now you own the lead forever. Use Mailchimp or GetResponse to send a 5-email sequence promoting all 5 products. Instagram traffic becomes a permanent email asset — immune to algorithm changes.
🛡️
Content-First Trust Building
Use the 80/20 rule: 80% of your Reels provide pure value (tips, tutorials, AI news, results breakdowns) with no selling. Only 20% are promotional. Instagram's algorithm rewards accounts that don't feel "salesy." When your audience trusts you as an AI authority, every promotional Reel converts at 3–5× higher rates than cold promotion.
🔗
Link Cloaking / Shorteners
Never use raw JVZoo links even in DMs. Use Pretty Links (WordPress plugin) or TinyURL with custom alias (e.g., yoursite.com/breakout-ai). Clean, branded links build trust and are never flagged. They also let you track clicks per product so you can double down on what's working.
