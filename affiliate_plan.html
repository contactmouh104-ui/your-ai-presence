The Stealth Link Strategy
Share affiliate links at scale without triggering spam filters or platform penalties
Instagram's algorithm actively suppresses accounts that look like they're selling. The solution: never look like you're selling. Build a system that makes people ASK you for the link.
🔗
The "Link in Bio" Bridge Page
Never link directly to JVZoo in your bio. JVZoo URLs are flagged as affiliate spam by Instagram's AI. Instead, use a bridge page — your own domain that pre-sells the offer before forwarding.

Bridge page structure:
  • Headline: "The [Tool] That Made Me [Result]"
  • 60-second story of your personal win
  • 3 bullet point benefits
  • CTA button → your JVZoo affiliate link
yourdomain.com/breakout-ai yourdomain.com/street-speak yourdomain.com/7-figure
Use Carrd, Systeme.io, or ClickFunnels to build these in under 20 minutes each.
🤖
DM Automation Keywords
Use ManyChat or Manychat-compatible tools to trigger automatic DMs when users comment a keyword on your Reel. This bypasses Instagram's link-in-comments ban entirely.

Example trigger setup:
Reel caption: "Comment 'BREAKOUT' and I'll send you the full case study link." Trigger keyword: BREAKOUT Auto-DM: "Hey [First Name]! Here's the Breakout AI case study link I mentioned: yourdomain.com/breakout-ai 🔥"
Pro tip: Use a different keyword for each product. This also builds your DM list for retargeting.
📲
The Story Swipe-Up Funnel
Instagram Stories with links have a much higher click-through rate than bio links. Use a 3-story sequence:

Story 1 (Tease): "I just discovered something insane. Watch until Story 3."
Story 2 (Proof): Screenshot or screen recording of results. Blur out sensitive info for authenticity.
Story 3 (Link): "Swipe up to get it →" pointing to your bridge page.

Post this sequence every 48 hours — rotate between your 5 products on a content calendar.
📧
The Email Bridge (Long-Term Asset)
Every DM keyword response should include a soft email opt-in. This builds an asset you own — immune to any platform ban.

Auto-DM (after link): "P.S. — I share exclusive tools and commissions-only strategies every week. Want me to add you to the list? Just reply 'YES' and I'll send your first one right now."
Tool stack: GetResponse or AWeber → 7-email sequence promoting all 5 products. One product per email. Soft sell, story-based format.
📅
The 7-Day Stealth Content Calendar
  1. Monday — Value Reel (No CTA): Pure education. "3 AI tools I use every day." No product mention. Builds trust. Algorithm rewards non-salesy content with reach.
  2. Tuesday — Story Sequence: Run the 3-story Tease → Proof → Link funnel for Product #1. Track DM keyword responses.
  3. Wednesday — Comment Bait Reel: Post the hook reel for Product #2. Caption: "Comment 'AIFLUENCERS' for the link." Watch ManyChat do the work.
  4. Thursday — Behind-the-Scenes Story: "Setting up my AI influencer today…" Soft tease. Builds curiosity for next day's content.
  5. Friday — Case Study Drop: Your strongest Reel — the Breakout AI $25K hook. This is your weekly traffic spike driver. Post at 6–8 PM in your target timezone.
  6. Saturday — Engagement Post: Ask a question in Stories or Reels: "What's your biggest struggle with online income?" Replies are future content ideas AND retargeting opportunities.
  7. Sunday — Retarget via DM: Follow up with everyone who triggered a keyword this week. Manually send a personalized 2-sentence message. Personal touch closes warm leads.

Originality & Branding System
Using StreetSpeak AI + Imimic to create original, penalty-proof content at scale
Instagram's "reused content" penalty kills reach for recycled or watermarked videos. The solution: use StreetSpeak AI's DFY videos as a base layer, then transform them into original content with Imimic voiceovers, custom text overlays, and unique B-roll cuts. The platform's fingerprinting algorithm looks for 3 things: audio originality, visual uniqueness, and metadata freshness — your workflow must address all three.
🎬
StreetSpeak AI — The Content Engine
Turn 1 DFY video into 5 original pieces
  • Download your base video — choose a StreetSpeak template that matches your product niche (AI/tech/wealth).
  • Remove the original audio track entirely — this is step one to defeating fingerprinting. Mute in CapCut or Premiere.
  • Change the first 2 and last 2 seconds — add a custom intro clip (even just your face for 1.5 seconds) and a custom outro. This creates a unique visual fingerprint.
  • Add text overlays in your brand font — use consistent colors, fonts, and positioning so your page has a recognizable "look" across all content.
  • Export at original quality — never compress or re-export more than once. Quality loss is a red flag in Instagram's content scoring.
🎙
Imimic — The Voice Layer
Human-like voiceovers that pass AI detection
  • Create your "signature voice" — pick ONE Imimic voice and stick to it across all your content. Consistency builds audio brand recognition.
  • Add natural imperfections — use Imimic's pacing controls to insert micro-pauses (0.3–0.5s) between sentences. Perfect AI speech is detectable; human speech breathes.
  • Layer background ambience — add low-volume café noise or keyboard sounds under your voiceover. This makes the audio unique at the frequency level.
  • Pitch-shift by 1–2% — use any free audio editor. Tiny pitch changes defeat audio fingerprinting 100% of the time without affecting perceived quality.
  • Match pacing to your hook — the first 3 seconds must be fast (urgency), the desire section slows down (emotional connection), the CTA speeds back up (momentum).
